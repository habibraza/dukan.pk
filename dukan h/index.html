How Dukan works

Create your own web store

Install Dukan on your mobile and submit basic information about yourself and your business. Get a customized web link to your store and share it with your potential customers.

What do you want to sell?

Follow a few easy steps in the app, upload pictures of your products, prices and other details.

Let’s start selling

Decide how your customers contact you, choose payment gateways, select delivery options, and start selling online with zero hassle.

Why Dukan

  • Inventory tracking

    Now you do not need to maintain a register to record your inventory. Dukan will not only keep a record of your products, it will also issue alerts when you are low on inventory.

  • Khata register

    Dukan maintains your customer and vendor credit management. By sending auto generated text messages, Dukan keeps your customers informed about what they owe you and lets them pay you back online.

  • Multiple delivery options

    To complete the process of online selling, Dukan offers in-app delivery services for your web store. Just a click away, Bykea and others will give you logistic support for getting your products to your customer’s door.

  • Accounting

    Dukan simplifies accounting for you and maintains basic records for how your business is performing. It will not only keep track of your business expenses and revenues, but will also generate sales reports for you to easily analyse your profitability.

  • Online payments

    From Cash on Delivery (COD) to automated Inter Bank Transfer (IBFT), Credit Card, Easypaisa, JazzCash, Dukan has a range of payment gateways that you can trust for your online transactions out of the box.

  • Point of sale (POS)

    With a flexible and easy-to-use Point of Sale (POS) interface, you can easily enter products or scan their barcodes to generate bills at your counter.
